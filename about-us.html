Industrial Lubricant Company in UAE

Petrotek has established itself as a trusted name among the top lubricant companies in the UAE. We specialize in the stocking, sourcing, blending, and global distribution of high-quality lubricants and engineering products tailored for automotive, marine, manufacturing, and industrial applications.

As a fast-growing lubricant company in Dubai and the Northern Emirates, we offer tailored lubrication solutions backed by technical expertise, strong logistics, and a customer-first mindset. Our operations span across the GCC and MENA region, delivering efficiency, quality, and reliability for a wide range of industries — from continuous production factories and precision engineering to hospitality and heavy-duty commercial sectors.

A Global Lubricant Presence, Rooted in UAE Excellence

Our capabilities extend beyond national borders, supplying premium oils, greases, and specialty lubricants to clients across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia. Petrotek is known for its dependable service, customized solutions, and flexible supply models. Whether you need daily-use engine oils or industry-specific lubricants, we ensure consistency and on-time delivery, powered by our local warehousing and dedicated fleet.

Furthermore, we have expanded our divisions to air, pumps, and CNC equipment. These divisions deliver comprehensive solutions, including supplies, spare parts, and services, bridging the gap between traditional and cutting-edge technologies in various industries.
