Petrotek is the top and leading lubricants supplier in the UAE, established in 2007 delivering a wide range of high-quality oils, greases, and industrial maintenance products to meet the needs of the automotive, manufacturing, marine, construction, and agricultural sectors. With a strong focus on innovation, continuous development, and customer satisfaction, our mission is to provide reliable lubrication solutions that enhance equipment performance and reduce operational costs.

We offer premium engine oils, hydraulic oils, gear lubricants, greases, food-grade oils (NSF certified), and specialty products tailored for both daily and heavy-duty industrial applications.
